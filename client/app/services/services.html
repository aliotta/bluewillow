Initial Consultation

45 minutes - 1 hr

You can expect to have a relaxed and detailed conversation with Bridget in your first visit in which the two of you discuss your physical, mental, and emotional health history and well-being. She will go through a comprehensive intake with you, touching upon your family and medical history. Bridget will gather as much information as possible in order to make the most informed suggestions about what herbal support would be right for you. Bridget may make nutritional, lifestyle, and herbal suggestions during your visit. After your visit, Bridget will compile the information you have given her and come up with a unique herbal formula for you, which you can pick up within a week.

Follow-Up Consultation

30 minutes

During a follow-up visit, Bridget will check in with you about your progress so far with the herbal protocol you agreed upon. She may make new suggestions or decide to change your formula, depending on how you are progressing. These consultations can be done over the phone.
