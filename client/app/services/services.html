Herbal Consultations - Initial

45 minutes - 1 hr, $60

You can expect to have a relaxed and detailed conversation with Bridget in your first visit in which the two of you discuss your physical, mental, and emotional health history and well-being. You will talk through a comprehensive intake, touching upon your family and medical history. Bridget will gather as much information as possible in order to make informed suggestions about what herbal support would be right for you. Bridget may make nutritional, lifestyle, and herbal recommendations during your visit. After your visit Bridget will develop a unique herbal formula and wellness plan based on your consultation, which you can pick up within a week.

Follow-Up Consultation

30 minutes, $35

During a follow-up visit, Bridget will check in with you about your progress. She may make new suggestions or decide to change your formula, depending on how you are progressing. These consultations can be done over the phone.
